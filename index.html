Get answers to common questions about Noord Pro

How is this different from existing trading platforms?

Noord Pro uniquely combines three specialized tools into one platform: real-time short monitoring, AI-powered Level II pattern explanation, and dark pool impact simulation. While other tools may offer one of these features, no platform connects all three with our cross-signal correlation engine.

What data sources do you use?

We aggregate data from multiple free financial APIs, including Financial Modeling Prep, Alpha Vantage, and Polygon/Massive for market data, supplemented with proprietary data normalization and enrichment. Our platform is designed to work with free-tier APIs during validation, with plans to integrate premium data feeds as we scale.
